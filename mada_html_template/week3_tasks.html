Task 1: Identify Key Narrative Points and Create a Plan

Narrative Plan

  • Objective 1: Educate users about common digital security threats.
  • Objective 2: Empower users to enhance their data privacy practices.
  • Objective 3: Discuss the implications of data privacy regulations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).
  • Conceptual Structure: Thematic approach focusing on practical application.
  • Narrative Voice: Informal and engaging, targeting tech-savvy individuals.

Reflective Statement

The narrative plan combines educational objectives with practical insights into digital security and data privacy. By focusing on audience needs and interests, the website aims to empower users with actionable knowledge while adhering to regulatory guidelines. Design considerations emphasise user-friendly navigation and engaging multimedia content to enhance learning experiences.
