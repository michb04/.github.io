Task 1: Analyse the Use of Video on the Web

Analysis of Video on Layer Creative Website

The Layer Creative website effectively uses video to showcase their portfolio and engage visitors. Here are some key points of analysis:

What are the videos used for?

  • Showcasing the company's work and portfolio.
  • Providing testimonials from clients.
  • Demonstrating the process and approach of the company.
  • Highlighting successful projects, including renowned games like Rocket League and Star Wars Battlefront.

Evidence of video used with intent:

  • Videos are strategically placed to provide visual evidence of the company's capabilities and previous projects.
  • Client testimonials are included in video format to enhance credibility and trust.
  • Showcases successful projects, emphasizing the company's impact and expertise in the gaming industry.

Benefits and caveats of video use:

  • Benefits: Engages visitors, provides a dynamic way to showcase work, and enhances information retention through visual storytelling.
  • Caveats: Videos may take longer to load, potentially impacting user experience for visitors with slower internet connections.

Suitability of video length:

  • The videos on Layer Creative's website are generally concise and to the point, ensuring that visitors remain engaged without feeling overwhelmed.

Combination with other media:

  • Videos are often combined with brief text descriptions and high-quality images to provide context and additional information.
  • Audio is used within videos to enhance the storytelling aspect and create an immersive experience.

Overall, the use of video on the Layer Creative website is well-executed, providing an engaging and informative experience for visitors. Videos are used purposefully to showcase the company's work, enhance credibility, and provide a dynamic element to the website. The inclusion of successful projects like Rocket League and Star Wars Battlefront further highlights the company's expertise and impact in the industry.
